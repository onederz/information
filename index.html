Grand Circuit

  • Read more▼

The Grand Circuit is a series of temples around the city of Angkor Wat. It is a popular tourist attraction because of its well-preserved architecture and historical significance. The temples were built in the 10th and 12th centuries and are some of Cambodia's most famous tourist destinations. We will visit Preah Khan, Neak Poun, Ta Som, Pre Rup, and Bakheng is the temple for the sunset.
